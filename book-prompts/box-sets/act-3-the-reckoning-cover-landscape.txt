CRITICAL COMPOSITION RULE — LANDSCAPE FORMAT:

The image is divided into THREE strict zones with NO overlap:

  ZONE 1 — TEXT BLOCK (top 0-18%): ALL text except author name. This zone contains ONLY text, decorative lines, and sky/atmospheric background. NO characters, NO weapons, NO figures of any kind may appear in this zone.

  ZONE 2 — CHARACTER ZONE (25-88%): All heroes rendered at a SMALLER scale than the portrait versions to ensure they fit entirely within this zone with comfortable margins above and below.   ZONE 3 — AUTHOR NAME (90-96%): ONLY the author name. Nothing else.

There must be a CLEAR VISUAL GAP between the bottom of the text block (Zone 1) and the top of any character's head (Zone 2). No text overlaps any character. No character overlaps any text.
