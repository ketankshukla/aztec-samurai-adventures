# Box Set Description - Act III: The Reckoning (Books 9-12)

## Amazon Book Description (HTML formatted)

<b>The blade is reforged. The heroes reunite. And the final war begins.</b>

<p>From the ashes of Act II's devastation, the alliance rises stronger than ever. The Sun-Blade is remade by the armorer who refused to let hope die. The heroes reunite, forge impossible alliances, and march on the Dominion capital for the battle that will decide the fate of all worlds.</p>

<p><b>This box set contains books 9-12 of the Aztec Samurai Adventures series:</b></p>

<ul>
<li><b>Book 9: The Forge of Souls - The Price of Power</b> -- The invisible armorer becomes the most important person in the war. The Sun-Blade is reforged from the souls of every hero who ever carried it. And the price of creation is everything Solkren has.</li>
<li><b>Book 10: The Mirror Queen - Realm of Shattered Glass</b> -- Neyla's healing gift becomes the key to unraveling the Dominion's entire infrastructure. But to free the enslaved thousands, she must enter the Mirror-Realm and face the assassin who kills with reflections.</li>
<li><b>Book 11: Crown of Stars - The Final Siege</b> -- The allied army storms the Dominion capital. Every remaining villain is confronted. The Starless Crown shatters. And the Gate must be closed from the inside.</li>
<li><b>Book 12: The Sun That Never Sets - Dawn of a New World</b> -- The war is over. The world remains. And the hardest part of any fight is learning to live in the peace you bled for.</li>
</ul>

<p>Act III: The Reckoning is the triumphant, cathartic conclusion to the series -- reunion, final war, sacrifice, and the legacy that outlasts everything. Every thread woven across twelve books comes together in an ending that is earned, bittersweet, and unforgettable.</p>

<p>For fans of <i>The Poppy War</i>, <i>The Blade Itself</i>, and <i>The Name of the Wind</i> -- epic fantasy where the most powerful weapon isn't the sharpest sword. It's the person holding it.</p>

<p><i>The sun is rising. And this time, it will never set.</i></p>
