# Chapter 10 Summary — SKYREN'S ENTRANCE

**POV:** Skyren (introduced)

Deep POV on Skyren's background and her flights through the storm. She's a Pyrrath golden hawk rider bonded to Cielovar for six years. Each rescue trip is described — diving through magical lightning, grabbing wounded soldiers, climbing out. Eleven lives saved.

The twelfth trip — lightning strikes Cielovar's wing directly. Skyren converts the uncontrolled descent into a controlled crash landing. Neyla heals the hawk's dislocated wing. Skyren is already planning the next flight.

**Key Events:** Skyren's backstory, eleven aerial rescues, lightning strike and crash, Neyla heals Cielovar, Skyren's personality established.

**Cliffhanger:** Cielovar screams at the sky. The hawk holds grudges. Soon they'll fly again.
