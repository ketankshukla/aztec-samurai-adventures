# Chapter 7 Summary — THE DIVERSION

**POV:** Kaelen

Group B executes the diversion — Kaelen uses shadow-step to appear at multiple positions, Rainara creates a false river that sweeps the eastern force, Skyren drops explosive charges on supply lines. The Dominion commits 2,000 soldiers to eastern pursuit.

Groups A and C escape. Group B flees east into wilderness — no supplies, no communication. Kaelen finds a sheltered hollow. Rainara collapses from exhaustion. Shadow-step erases their trail.

**Key Events:** Eastern diversion executed, false river created, 2,000 Dominion soldiers drawn east, Group B escapes into wilderness, trail erased.

**Cliffhanger:** Three people stranded in wilderness with 2,000 hunters searching the forest. The base burns behind them.
