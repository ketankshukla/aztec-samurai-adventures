# Chapter 8 Summary — GROUP A EVACUATES

**POV:** Itzil

Itzil leads 4,000 soldiers through the northern tunnel — fighting retreat through mountain terrain. Jagren commands the rearguard with cold efficiency. Torvane manages logistics. Tunnel entrance collapsed behind them.
