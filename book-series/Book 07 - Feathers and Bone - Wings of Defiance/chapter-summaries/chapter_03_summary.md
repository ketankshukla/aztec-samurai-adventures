# Chapter 3 Summary — SKYREN'S MISSION

**POV:** Skyren

Skyren flies Miyako south to Neyla's group. Two-day flight, camping on a cliff edge. Unexpected connection — both know what it's like to survive when others don't. Miyako: "The picture looks different from up close. Don't forget to land."

They reach Neyla's group. Miyako embraces Zariel and Neyla. Looks south toward Kanezawa. "How far?" "Two days' march." "Then we march."

**Key Events:** Two-day flight south, Skyren-Miyako bonding, "don't forget to land" lesson, arrival at Coravel camp, march south begins.

**Cliffhanger:** Three people march toward a city that is beautiful and enslaved and waiting.
