# Chapter 8 Summary — HELISAR'S FASCINATION

**POV:** Helisar (Villain)

Helisar discovers the reversals — not angry but FASCINATED. "This is healing magic. Someone is healing the oath away." He wants to study the healer, not kill her: "She's the most interesting thing that's happened to my work in years."

A freed person left four words scratched into the wall: "I REMEMBER MY NAME." Helisar stares at it for a very long time. First crack in his certainty. He chooses not to have the words cleaned.

**Key Events:** Helisar's fascination with reversal technique, orders healer captured alive, "I REMEMBER MY NAME" message, first crack in certainty.

**Cliffhanger:** The crack in the wall. The crack in the certainty. He leaves the words there.
