# Dramatis Personae

## The Alliance

- **Itzil** — Sun-Blade bearer, commander. Leading the race for the Sunheart relic at the original Sun-Blade temple.
- **Kaelen** — Scout and infiltrator. Forward scout on the southern march. Miyako's student, carrying her last lessons.
- **Jagren** — Duelist and rearguard commander. The boy who fought for glory became the man who fights for people.
- **Neyla** — Healer. Ash-oath reversal specialist. Frees over two hundred people at Kanezawa before reaching her limit.
- **Torvane** — Engineer. Managing logistics for the army's southern march.
- **Miyako** — Shadow school master. Focus character. Returns to her home city Kanezawa. Destroys the ash-oath anchor. The first beloved hero to die.
- **Amalura** — Lorekeeper. Still captive in the Dominion detention tower.
- **Korvain** — Itzil's mentor. On a stretcher, declining. Two old warriors share their final twilight.
- **Zariel** — Diplomat-spymaster. Coordinates the Kanezawa infiltration and evacuation.
- **Skyren** — Hawk rider. Focus character. The thread connecting the scattered alliance. Coordinates aerial evacuation at Kanezawa.
- **Rainara** — Water-knot mystic. Water-magic support for the army's southern march.
- **Solkren** — Armorer. Quiet, present, working. The armorer no one is watching.

## The Dominion

- **Volzentar** — Emperor. Directing the race for the Sunheart relic.
- **Valdremor** — The Architect. Extracting relic locations from Amalura's copied knowledge. His forces race south.
- **Nightshade** — Spymaster. Hunting Neyla as priority target.
- **Helisar** — Ash-oath master. Operating the Kanezawa factory. Fascinated by Neyla's reversal technique. Watches his life's work destroyed.
- **Serenthar (The Weeping One)** — Demon-bound seer. Her prophecy drives the endgame.
