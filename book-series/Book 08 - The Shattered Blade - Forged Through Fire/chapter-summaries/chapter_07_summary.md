# Chapter 7 Summary — THE BATTLE FOR THE TEMPLE

**POV:** Kaelen

Full battle at the temple ruins. Kaelen coordinates defense during Itzil's trial. Jagren holds the south — a masterclass in efficient, protective fighting. Torvane's traps devastate the east. Rainara creates water-barriers west.

Itzil emerges from the temple — Sun-Blade blazing, Sunheart in hand. Alliance cheers. Dominion falters. Then Nightshade: "There she is. Break her."

**Key Events:** Six-hour defensive battle, three-sector defense, Itzil's emergence with relic, Dominion assault redirects to bearer.

**Cliffhanger:** The alliance's hope emerges. The Dominion's plan activates.
