# Dramatis Personae

## The Alliance

- **Itzil** — Sun-Blade bearer, commander. Focus character. Faces the temple trial, loses the blade, loses Korvain, and discovers she is more than what she carries.
- **Kaelen** — Scout and infiltrator. Defends the temple, covers Itzil's retreat, speaks the words that pull her back from the edge. First kiss.
- **Jagren** — Duelist and rearguard commander. Fights the surviving Ashvanar brother. The wall Itzil needs him to be.
- **Neyla** — Healer. Ash-oath reversal now reliable. Watches Korvain fade.
- **Torvane** — Engineer. Defends the temple with traps, mines, and mechanical crossbows.
- **Amalura** — Lorekeeper. Still captive. Rescue planned for Book 9.
- **Korvain** — Itzil's mentor. Gives his final lesson: "The blade broke. Good." Dies peacefully in his sleep.
- **Zariel** — Diplomat-spymaster. Maintains intelligence network.
- **Skyren** — Hawk rider. Aerial reconnaissance and intelligence.
- **Rainara** — Water-knot mystic. Creates water-barriers in the temple battle.
- **Solkren** — Armorer. Discovers the blade fragments still resonate. "I see you." The seed of Book 9.

## The Dominion

- **Volzentar** — Emperor. Issues surrender ultimatum. The sky cracks above his capital.
- **Valdremor** — The Architect. Accelerates the Great Gate. First flicker of doubt.
- **Nightshade** — Spymaster. Deploys blood-magic corrosion spell to shatter the Sun-Blade. Takes a fragment as trophy.
- **The Ashvanar Brother** — Surviving sorcerer. Berserk revenge for his sibling killed in Book 4. Wounded by Jagren.
- **Serenthar (The Weeping One)** — Demon-bound seer. Weeps louder as futures converge.
