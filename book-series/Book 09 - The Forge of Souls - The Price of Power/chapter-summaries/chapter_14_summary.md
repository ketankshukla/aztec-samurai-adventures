# Chapter 14 Summary — NIGHTSHADE'S FURY

**POV:** Nightshade (Villain)

Nightshade discovers Amalura's escape. Furious — this was her personal responsibility. Pursues via blood-portals. Catches them at a river crossing.

Nightshade vs. Kaelen: brief, intense duel. She's stronger magically. He's faster physically. Neither wins — a draw. Kaelen buys enough time. Nightshade at the riverbank: "This isn't over." Kaelen: "It never is."

**Key Events:** Nightshade's fury, blood-portal pursuit, river crossing duel, tactical draw, professional recognition between enemies.

**Cliffhanger:** She doesn't cross the river. The balance has shifted.
