# Dramatis Personae

## The Alliance

- **Solkren** — Armorer. Focus character. The quiet man no one watched becomes the most important person in the war. Discovers the soul-forge technique and reforges the Sun-Blade from nine heroes' souls.
- **Itzil** — Commander. Contributes the first soul-memory: her need for a mentor. Wields the reforged blade.
- **Kaelen** — Scout. Leads the Amalura rescue. Contributes his fear of belonging.
- **Jagren** — Duelist. Fights the extraction. Contributes his need to be seen.
- **Neyla** — Healer. Contributes her paralyzing grief. Ash-oath reversal now reliable at scale.
- **Amalura** — Lorekeeper. Rescued from captivity. Reveals the errors she planted in Valdremor's copied knowledge. Returns as strategic advisor.
- **Zariel** — Diplomat-spymaster. Contributes his need to always have the right answer.
- **Skyren** — Hawk rider. Contributes her need to run from everything below.
- **Rainara** — Water-knot mystic. Contributes her rage. Enables remote soul-contributions and rescue infiltration.
- **Torvane** — Engineer. Contributes his need to control every variable.

## The Dominion

- **Volzentar** — Emperor. The Dominion's figurehead as the Gate approaches completion.
- **Valdremor** — The Architect. Loses composure for the first time when Amalura escapes and the blade is reforged. Orders: "Burn them all. Open the Gate."
- **Nightshade** — Spymaster. Pursues the rescue team. Duels Kaelen to a draw at the river.
- **Serenthar (The Weeping One)** — Demon-bound seer. Her prophecy about "the armorer no one watches" comes true.

## The Entity

- **VASTRIX** — The hunger beyond the Gate. Projects its voice across the continent: "I SEE YOU. ALL OF YOU. AND I AM HUNGRY."
