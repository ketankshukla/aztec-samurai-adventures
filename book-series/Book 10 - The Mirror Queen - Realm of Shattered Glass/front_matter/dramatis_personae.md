# Dramatis Personae

## The Alliance

- **Neyla** — Healer. Focus character. Her ash-oath reversal technique becomes the key to unraveling the Dominion's infrastructure. Enters the Mirror-Realm to free trapped souls en masse. Gains soul-sight after the Mirror-Realm.
- **Itzil** — Commander. Wields the reforged blade. Leads the siege of the Dominion capital.
- **Kaelen** — Scout. Detects Mirathane's mirror-strikes. Coordinates the army's advance as a general in all but name.
- **Jagren** — Duelist. Leads the breach team at the siege. Prays for the first time on the eve of battle.
- **Rainara** — Water-knot mystic. Enters the Mirror-Realm with Neyla. Fights Mirathane in the assassin's own domain — water vs. mirrors. Develops water-scrying ability.
- **Amalura** — Lorekeeper. Explains the Mirror-Realm and Mirathane's nature. Strategic advisor.
- **Skyren** — Hawk rider. Final aerial reconnaissance of the Dominion capital. Maps the assault route.
- **Zariel** — Diplomat-spymaster. Manages Dominion surrenders and defections. Disinformation campaign bears fruit.
- **Torvane** — Engineer. Builds siege engines, ward-disruptors, and mobile ward-shields for the assault.
- **Solkren** — Armorer. Maintains the army's equipment through the campaign.

## The Dominion

- **Volzentar** — Emperor. Presides over the crumbling empire.
- **Valdremor** — The Architect. Prepares the capital's defense with flawed data. Visits Serenthar one final time.
- **Nightshade** — Spymaster. Commands blood-ward soldiers in the capital's defense.
- **Mirathane** — Mirror-Realm assassin. Demon-bonded. Kills through reflections. Deployed to assassinate Neyla. Trapped in her own dimension.
- **Gravos** — Surviving Ashvanar brother. Commands a sorcery tower in the capital's defense.
- **Helisar** — Ash-oath creator. Sends a cryptic message suggesting doubt: "I'm beginning to wonder if peace was the wrong word for what I built."
- **Serenthar (The Weeping One)** — Demon-bound seer. Waits beneath the palace. "In both futures, you wish you'd done things differently."

## The Entity

- **VASTRIX** — The hunger beyond the Gate. Pressing harder as the Gate weakens. Screams when souls are freed. A dimension made of hunger.
