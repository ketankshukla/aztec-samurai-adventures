# Dramatis Personae

## The Alliance

- **Itzil** — Commander. Focus character. Wields the reforged blade against Vastrix. Leads the final assault on the Great Gate.
- **Kaelen** — Scout-general. Coordinates the urban advance through the capital. "A house somewhere. With a view."
- **Jagren** — Duelist. Leads the right flank. Cuts Vastrix's tendrils. Volunteers for the Gate. "Let me fight for something real."
- **Neyla** — Healer. Works behind the lines reversing blood-wards and healing the wounded. Soul-sight guides the final battle.
- **Rainara** — Water-knot mystic. Covers the left flank. Drowns dark energy in water. Fights Vastrix's manifestations.
- **Amalura** — Lorekeeper. Counters Valdremor's final trap. Outsmarts the Architect. Prepares Serenthar's ritual.
- **Skyren** — Hawk rider. Aerial support during the Gate plaza battle. Drops signal charges.
- **Zariel** — Diplomat-spymaster. Commands the full army during the final assault.
- **Torvane** — Engineer. Defeats the Ashvanar's sorcery tower and Dalrignon's portal field — with math.
- **Solkren** — Armorer. Stands with Itzil, ready to maintain the blade. Reinforces it during the Vastrix confrontation.

## The Dominion — Final Fates

- **Nightshade** — Spymaster. Duels Itzil on the inner wall. Blood-ward network severed. Offered mercy. Chooses to fall. Sixth villain defeat.
- **Gravos** — Surviving Ashvanar brother. Mad with grief. Fortifies a sorcery tower. Torvane turns his power against him. Seventh villain defeat.
- **Valdremor** — The Architect. Discovers Amalura's planted errors. Offers a deal. Refused. Activates a disintegration ward. Amalura counters it. Captured. Eighth villain defeat.
- **Dalrignon** — Portal engineer. Fortifies the Gate plaza with micro-portals. Torvane inverts them. Consumed by his own portal collapse. Ninth villain defeat.
- **Helisar** — Ash-oath creator. Turns against the Dominion when he sees Vastrix. Helps Neyla break the last enslaved souls. Dies redeeming himself. Tenth villain defeat.
- **Pearlvaine** — Diplomat. Tries to negotiate with Vastrix. Consumed. Eleventh villain defeat.
- **Relicara** — Relic collector. Tries to steal Vastrix's power. Crushed. Twelfth villain defeat.

## The Oracle

- **Serenthar (The Weeping One)** — Demon-bound seer. Reveals she originally opened the Gate centuries ago. Volunteers to close it from inside — a one-way trip. Shatters the keystone. The Gate collapses. Serenthar does not return.

## The Entity

- **VASTRIX** — The hunger beyond the Gate. Half-manifested. Caught in the collapsing Gate. Destroyed by the reforged blade — nine soul-threads blazing. Dissolves into golden light. The Starless Crown shatters.
