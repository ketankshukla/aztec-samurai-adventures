# Chapter 3 Summary — NEYLA'S LAST PATIENTS

**POV:** Neyla

Medical aftermath — thousands wounded. Ash-oath reversals continue; many dissolve on their own with Gate destroyed. Neyla finds the last ash-oath factory. Frees dozens one by one.

A freed woman: "I remember my daughter's name." Neyla: "Tell me." The woman says it. Neyla repeats it. The woman smiles. Neyla sleeps for first time in three days. Her legacy: not just her hands, but the hands she taught.

**Key Events:** Last ash-oath factory found, final slaves freed, legacy of trained healers.
