# Chapter 21 Summary — THE YOUNG WARRIOR

**POV:** Itzil

Seven years after the war. Passing the torch. A young person stands before Itzil — talented, scared, unsure. The way Itzil stood before Korvain.

"How do you know you're ready?" Itzil smiles — Korvain's smile. "You don't. You just show up." The young person walks into the academy. Itzil touches the blade: "One more." The torch passes.

**Key Events:** New student arrives, Korvain's lesson transmitted, torch passed, cycle continues.
