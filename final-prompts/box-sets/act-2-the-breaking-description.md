# Box Set Description - Act II: The Breaking (Books 5-8)

## Amazon Book Description (HTML formatted)

<b>The Dominion strikes back. The heroes are scattered. And everything they built is about to break.</b>

<p>The alliance won its first battles. Now the empire answers with overwhelming force, impossible technology, and a darkness that devours the stars themselves. The heroes are separated, tested individually, broken -- and reforged into something the Dominion never expected.</p>

<p><b>This box set contains books 5-8 of the Aztec Samurai Adventures series:</b></p>

<ul>
<li><b>Book 5: The Serpent's Gambit - A Spy Among Shadows</b> -- Kaelen goes deeper undercover than any operative has ever gone, into the beating heart of the Dominion's intelligence apparatus. The longer he wears the mask, the better it fits.</li>
<li><b>Book 6: Rain of Obsidian - Tides of Dark Magic</b> -- The sky rains glass. The rivers run with poison. The alliance's hidden base is destroyed. And a water mystic discovers she can purify what the Dominion corrupts.</li>
<li><b>Book 7: Feathers and Bone - Wings of Defiance</b> -- The alliance is shattered across a continent. A hawk rider becomes the thread connecting the broken pieces. And the first beloved hero pays the ultimate price.</li>
<li><b>Book 8: The Shattered Blade - Forged Through Fire</b> -- Itzil's Sun-Blade shatters. Her mentor dies. Everything she relied on is gone. From the ashes, she discovers she was never defined by what she held -- but by who she is.</li>
</ul>

<p>Act II: The Breaking is the emotional crucible of the series -- the books where heroes are separated, tested beyond their limits, and transformed by loss into something stronger than they were before.</p>

<p>For fans of <i>The Poppy War</i>, <i>The Blade Itself</i>, and <i>The Name of the Wind</i> -- epic fantasy where the cost of war is measured not in battles but in the people you lose along the way.</p>

<p><i>The blade is broken. The fire is not out. It just needs someone willing to hold the heat.</i></p>

<p><b>Books 5-8 of 12 in the <i>Aztec Samurai Adventures</i> series. Over 140,000 words of epic fantasy adventure. From Ketan Shukla, published by Metronagon.</b></p>
