# Box Set Description - The Complete Series (Books 1-12)

## Amazon Book Description (HTML formatted)

<b>Twelve books. One warrior. The complete epic fantasy saga that combines the warrior traditions of Mesoamerica with the disciplined philosophy of the samurai.</b>

<p>When the Dominion raids her temple and steals a sacred relic, warrior Itzil is thrust into a war she never expected -- armed with a blade of pure sunlight that hasn't manifested in three hundred years. Over twelve books, she will gather allies, lose everything, rebuild from the ashes, and lead an army against the greatest evil the world has ever known.</p>

<p><b>This complete collection contains all twelve books in the Aztec Samurai Adventures series:</b></p>

<p><b>Act I - The Rising (Books 1-4):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Sunblade Rising - A Blade Forged in Light</li>
<li>The Mirror Siege - Reflections of Betrayal</li>
<li>Ash Oaths - Bonds Written in Blood</li>
<li>The Starless Crown - The Darkness Unveiled</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Act II - The Breaking (Books 5-8):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The Serpent's Gambit - A Spy Among Shadows</li>
<li>Rain of Obsidian - Tides of Dark Magic</li>
<li>Feathers and Bone - Wings of Defiance</li>
<li>The Shattered Blade - Forged Through Fire</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Act III - The Reckoning (Books 9-12):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The Forge of Souls - The Price of Power</li>
<li>The Mirror Queen - Realm of Shattered Glass</li>
<li>Crown of Stars - The Final Siege</li>
<li>The Sun That Never Sets - Dawn of a New World</li>
</ul>

<p>From the golden temples where the Sun-Blade first blazes to life, through the mirror-portals and poisoned rivers of a continent at war, to the final siege of the Dominion capital and the dawn of a new world -- this is the complete journey. Every battle. Every betrayal. Every kiss. Every sacrifice. Every sunrise.</p>

<p><b>In this series:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A reluctant warrior who discovers she can manifest a blade of pure sunlight -- and that the blade responds not to strength, but to clarity of purpose</li>
<li>A team of broken, brilliant people who become a family forged in fire: a bitter scout, a flashy duelist, a quiet engineer, a healer with impossible abilities, a hawk rider, a water mystic, a diplomat, an armorer, and an exile sensei</li>
<li>A war against an empire built on stolen souls -- and something far worse hiding behind it</li>
<li>A love story that grows from distrust to partnership to something neither person expected</li>
<li>Villains who believe they are right -- and one who might actually be</li>
<li>A world where the most powerful weapon isn't the sharpest sword. It's the courage to show up when everything says you shouldn't.</li>
</ul>

<p>For fans of <i>The Poppy War</i>, <i>The Blade Itself</i>, <i>The Name of the Wind</i>, and <i>Avatar: The Last Airbender</i> -- epic fantasy at its most ambitious, most emotional, and most human.</p>

<p><i>The sun is rising. The blade is lit. And the story of a lifetime awaits.</i></p>
